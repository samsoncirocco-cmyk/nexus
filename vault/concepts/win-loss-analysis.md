### What worked?
- What was the primary reason they chose Fortinet?
- Which features/capabilities sealed the deal?
- What did you do well in the sales process?
- Who was your champion and how did you develop them?

### What almost went wrong?
- Any moments where the deal was at risk?
- Objections that were tough to overcome?
- Competitors that made it close?

### Replicable plays
- What from this deal can you repeat on similar accounts?
- Templates, demos, or talk tracks that landed?

## Quarterly Trends

Review your win/loss analyses quarterly. Look for patterns:

- **Win rate by segment** — Where are you strongest?
- **Common loss reasons** — What keeps coming up?
- **Competitor patterns** — Who do you lose to most and why?
- **Cycle length trends** — Are deals getting faster or slower?
- **Deal size trends** — Moving upmarket or staying flat?

Use these patterns to adjust your territory strategy and focus.
