# State Contracts & Procurement Vehicles

## National Cooperatives

| Cooperative | Contract # | Coverage | Notes |
|------------|-----------|---------|-------|
| **NASPO ValuePoint** | Check portal | All 50 states | Gold standard for state procurement |
| **OMNIA Partners** | Check portal | National | Strong in local gov + education |
| **Sourcewell** | Check portal | National | Very popular in K-12. Easy registration |
| **NCPA** | Check portal | National | Growing presence |
| **HGACBuy** | Check portal | 26+ states | Expanding |

## Iowa

### State Procurement
- **Fiscal Year:** July 1 – June 30
- **Competitive Bid Threshold:** Varies by agency (typically $50K+)
- **State IT Contract:** Check Iowa DAS procurement portal
- **E-Rate consultants in territory:** David Fringer (iowaaea.org), Gina Rogers (gwaea.org)

### Key Procurement Contacts
- Iowa Department of Administrative Services (DAS)
- Iowa Communications Network (ICN) — supports K-12 connectivity

## Nebraska

### State Procurement
- **Fiscal Year:** July 1 – June 30 (biennial budget)
- **Competitive Bid Threshold:** Varies
- **Key E-Rate consultant:** Natalie Coffin/Brauer (esu5.org) — manages MANY NE school RFPs
- **Sarah Pesek** (esu10.org) — central/western NE schools

### Notable
- ESU5 (Natalie Coffin) handles E-Rate for a huge number of NE schools
- Many NE school RFPs are templated similarly — Fortinet-first language common
- ESU10 (Sarah Pesek) covers a different region with Aruba-first language

## South Dakota

### State Procurement
- **Fiscal Year:** July 1 – June 30
- **Key E-Rate consultant:** Bonnie Overweg (dakotaacademic.com), Erin Johnson (eratecomplete.com)
- **BIT (Bureau of Information & Telecommunications):** Central IT for state agencies

### Notable
- Smaller territory (242 accounts)
- Less E-Rate activity than IA or NE
- Strong Meraki presence in schools

## E-Rate Filing Calendar (All States)

| Phase | Timing | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Planning | Jul-Sep | Assess needs, identify projects |
| Form 470 | Oct-Jan | Open competition (must post before purchasing) |
| Evaluation | Jan-Mar | Review bids, select vendor |
| Form 471 | Jan-Mar | Apply for funding |
| FCDL | Apr-Jun | Receive Funding Commitment Decision Letter |
| Deployment | Jun-Aug | Install during summer break |
| Operational | Sep | Ready for new school year |
